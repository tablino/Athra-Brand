٠١ · What We Do

Five layers, one institutional relationship.

The programmes are how we are paid. Trust is what we are actually building. Each layer below earns the next — and most engagements span more than one.
٠١
— primary revenue layer —

Custom Institutional Programmes

Bespoke executive development designed around a single institution's strategic agenda. Typically six to eighteen months, with cohorts of twelve to twenty senior leaders, blended in-person and remote, delivered in Arabic, English, or both.

For sovereign-linked organisations, banks, ministries, large corporates.
٠٢
— authority & visibility —

Open Enrolment

Public-facing programmes on focused topics — governance, succession, executive presence, fintech leadership. Smaller cohorts of fifteen to twenty-five, drawn from across the region. The front door to the platform.

Open application. Two intakes per year, in Dubai and Riyadh.
٠٣
— credibility layer —

Certifications &
Partner Programmes

Delivered with institutional partners including CMT, ACI, and select global business schools. Certifications carry recognised credentials and serve as institutional trust signals for organisations placing their senior teams.

Eight active partnerships across finance and leadership disciplines.
٠٤
— relationship infrastructure —

Executive Forums &
Convenings

Invite-only roundtables, sector councils, family enterprise forums, and private leadership dinners. The room itself is the deliverable. Held three to four times annually across the GCC, by invitation.

No press, no recordings, no public attendee lists.
٠٥
— long-term engagement —

Advisory-Learning Engagements

For institutions building permanent internal capability infrastructure — corporate academies, in-house executive programmes, succession architectures. Athra advises on design, builds the faculty network, and runs the first one or two cohorts before handing over. Typical engagement: eighteen to thirty-six months.

Reserved for institutions willing to make a multi-year commitment to capability.
